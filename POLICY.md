# LibreFind Privacy Policy

Last updated: **10.02.2026**

## User data sent to Supabase

The following data is collected:
- A randomly created device id
- The total count of apps, the count of FOSS apps, the count of proprietary apps and the count of unknown apps

If you create a account also the following data is collected:
- Username
- Email
- Encrypted password
- Your submissions
