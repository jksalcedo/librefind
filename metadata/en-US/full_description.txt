LibreFind is a free and lightweight Android app that scans your device, identifies proprietary software, and recommends Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) alternatives.

<b>Core Features</b>

• Scan installed apps to detect proprietary software
• Get personalized FOSS alternative recommendations
• View sovereignty scores showing FOSS vs proprietary ratio
• Rate and review FOSS alternatives
• Community-driven database

<b>Community Contributions</b>

• Propose new FOSS alternatives for review
• Submit pros and cons for existing alternatives
• Vote on app recommendations
• Help build a comprehensive alternative database

<b>Privacy First</b>

All app scanning happens locally on your device. The app only queries the database for FOSS alternatives when proprietary apps are detected.

<b>Open Source</b>

LibreFind is fully open source under the MIT License. Contribute at: https://github.com/jksalcedo/librefind

<b>Required Permissions</b>

• QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES - To scan installed apps on your device
• INTERNET - To fetch FOSS alternative recommendations from the database
